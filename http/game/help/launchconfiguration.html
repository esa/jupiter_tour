Launch configuration

In the slider associated to this icon, you can select the bounds for the launch epoch: that is the launch window. The numbers indicates the Modified Julian Date and are, essentially, days.
In the slider associated to this icon, you can select bounds for launch DV in m/s. This number is, essentially, the initial velocity of the spacecraft provided at launch.
In the slider associated to this icon, you can select bounds for the time of flight (in days) of the trajectory leg. The slider also shows some vertical lines. These lines are placed at the resonant periods. If you select as next target the current planet/moon, by bounding the time of flight around these lines you will most probably obtain a resonant transfer.
